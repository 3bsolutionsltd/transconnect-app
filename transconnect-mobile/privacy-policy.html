Privacy Policy for TransConnect

Last Updated: December 27, 2025

Introduction

TransConnect ("we," "our," or "us") operates the TransConnect mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Booking Information: Travel routes, dates, seat preferences, payment details
  • Profile Information: Optional profile picture and preferences

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, pages viewed, time spent in the App
  • Location Data: Approximate location to show nearby routes and stops (only when permitted)

Permissions Used

Camera Permission

We request camera access to:

  • Scan QR codes for ticket verification
  • Capture profile pictures (optional)

You can deny this permission and the App will still function, but you won't be able to scan QR codes or upload profile pictures.

Location Permission

We request location access to:

  • Show nearby bus stops and routes
  • Provide location-based search results
  • Improve route recommendations

You can deny this permission and manually enter your location.

Storage Permission

We may request storage access to:

  • Save tickets for offline access
  • Cache route information
  • Store profile pictures locally

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and manage your bus ticket bookings
  • Send booking confirmations and trip reminders
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve App functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

Payment Information

We do not store payment card details. All mobile money transactions are processed securely through:

  • MTN Mobile Money
  • Airtel Money
  • Flutterwave (payment gateway)

Payment information is handled by these third-party payment processors in compliance with industry standards.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Bus Operators: To fulfill your bookings
  • Payment Processors: To process transactions securely
  • Service Providers: For analytics, notifications, and app functionality
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect rights and safety

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication (JWT tokens)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as:

  • Your account is active
  • Needed to provide services
  • Required by law or business purposes

You can request deletion of your account and data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent for location/camera permissions at any time

Children's Privacy

TransConnect is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

Our App may contain links to third-party services (bus operator websites, payment gateways). We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the App with an updated "Last Updated" date.
