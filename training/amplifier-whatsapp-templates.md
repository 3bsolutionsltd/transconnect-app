## 3. Booking Assistance

### How to Make a Booking
```
Booking your seat is super easy! Here's how:

1. *Select Route:* Tap "Book Now" and choose:
   • From: [e.g., Kampala]
   • To: [e.g., Jinja]
   • Date: [Select travel date]

2. *Choose Bus:* Pick your preferred departure time and bus company

3. *Select Seat:* Tap available seats (green) on the seat map

4. *Passenger Info:* Confirm your details

5. *Payment:* Choose Mobile Money or Card
   • MTN: Dial *165# to approve
   • Airtel: Dial *185# to approve

6. *Get Ticket:* Your QR code ticket arrives instantly! 🎫

Questions on any step? I'm here to help! 😊
```

### Seat Selection Confusion
```
Let me explain the seat map colors:

🟢 *Green = Available* → You can book these
🔴 *Red = Taken* → Already booked
⚪ *Gray = Reserved* → Bus company reserved (driver, attendant)

*To select your seat:*
1. Tap any green seat you prefer
2. Window seats: Columns A & D (best views!)
3. Aisle seats: Columns B & C (easy access)
4. Front seats: Rows 1-5 (less bumpy ride)

Which seat would you like? I can help you choose! 🚌
```

### Change/Cancel Booking
```
I can help with that!

*To cancel or modify:*
1. Open TransConnect app
2. Go to "My Bookings"
3. Select your trip
4. Tap "Cancel" or "Modify"

*Important:*
```

## 4. Payment Issues

### Payment Failed
```
Sorry you're experiencing payment issues! Let's fix this.

*Common solutions:*

*For MTN Mobile Money:*
1. Check your balance (dial *165#)
2. Ensure name on account matches booking name
3. Wait for USSD prompt (*165#) and approve within 2 minutes
4. Check daily transaction limit (default: 1,000,000 UGX)

*For Airtel Money:*
1. Check balance (dial *185#)
2. Approve transaction when prompted
3. Check if PIN is correct

Still failing? Tell me:
• Which payment method?
• Error message shown?

I'll help you resolve this ASAP! 💪
```

```
*If you see payment deducted:*

• Screenshot your payment confirmation

Still no ticket after 5 minutes? Send me:
• Screenshot of payment confirmation
• Your phone number used for booking

I'll escalate this to our tech team immediately! 🚨
```

## Best Practices

### Personalization Tips
- ✅ Always use customer's name
- ✅ Reference specific details (route, time, amount)
- ✅ Adjust tone based on situation (urgent vs. casual)
- ✅ Add relevant emojis (but don't overdo it)

### Response Quality
- ✅ Read full customer message before selecting template
- ✅ Combine templates if needed for complex issues
- ✅ Always offer next step or ask if more help needed
- ✅ Use "I" and "we" (humanize the interaction)

### What to Avoid
- ❌ Copy-paste without personalizing
- ❌ Template doesn't fully match customer's question
- ❌ Too formal or robotic
- ❌ No clear call-to-action
- ❌ Leaving customer hanging (always close)
