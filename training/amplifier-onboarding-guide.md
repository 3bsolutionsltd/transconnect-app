# Onboarding Guide: Digital Marketing & Support Specialist

**Last Updated:** July 2, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Target Role:** Digital Marketing & Support Specialist ("The Amplifier")

## Overview

Welcome to TransConnect! As the Digital Marketing & Support Specialist, you are the voice and reach of our platform. This guide will help you transition smoothly into your role during your first two weeks, ensuring you understand our systems, meet key stakeholders, and begin driving user acquisition while delivering exceptional customer support.

**Your Mission:** Drive qualified app installs through digital channels while maintaining customer trust and reducing funnel drop-off from download to first booking.

## Quick Reference: Daily Workflow (After Onboarding)

### Morning Routine (9:00 AM - 10:00 AM)
1. Check overnight WhatsApp messages (respond immediately)
2. Review ad campaign performance from previous day
3. Check Play Store/social media for reviews and comments
4. Review Google Analytics for conversion trends
5. Plan daily priorities

### Active Hours (10:00 AM - 5:00 PM)
- Monitor WhatsApp continuously (10-min response target during peaks)
- Make campaign optimizations as needed
- Create/schedule social content per calendar
- Engage with social media community
- Coordinate with field team for content/feedback

### End-of-Day (5:00 PM - 5:30 PM)
- Log daily metrics (installs, CPI, response times, bookings)
- Respond to any pending inquiries
- Flag issues for next-day follow-up
- Brief Technical Lead on significant developments

### Weekly (Every Friday)
- Compile weekly campaign report
- Review and present KPIs to Technical Lead
- Plan campaigns and content for next week
- Update templates/processes based on learnings
