# Daily Workflow: Digital Marketing & Support Specialist

**Last Updated:** July 3, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Target Role:** Digital Marketing & Support Specialist ("The Amplifier")

## Overview

This guide outlines your daily workflow to ensure consistent performance and KPI achievement. Follow this structure while remaining flexible for urgent issues or opportunities.

---

## Morning Routine (9:00 AM - 10:00 AM)

### 9:00 AM - System Check & Overnight Review
- [ ] **Check WhatsApp Business** for overnight messages
  - Respond to all urgent inquiries immediately
  - Flag technical issues for escalation
  - Typical volume: 5-15 messages overnight
  
- [ ] **Review campaign performance** (previous day)
  - Meta Ads Manager: Check CPI, impressions, installs
  - Google Ads: Review Universal App Campaign metrics
  - Flag any campaigns with CPI > $0.60 for optimization
  
- [ ] **Monitor app store presence**
  - Play Store: Check new reviews (respond within 24 hours)
  - Star rating trend: Ensure maintaining 4.0+
  - Screenshot any negative reviews for analysis

- [ ] **Check social media**
  - Facebook: Respond to comments on posts
  - Instagram: Engage with DMs and story replies
  - Twitter/X: Monitor brand mentions
  
### 9:30 AM - Analytics Deep Dive
- [ ] **Google Analytics review**
  - Yesterday's installs and active users
  - Download-to-booking conversion rate
  - Drop-off points in user journey
  - Top traffic sources
  
- [ ] **Dashboard metrics** (if available)
  - Total bookings generated from digital channels
  - Revenue attributed to your campaigns
  - Geographic breakdown of users
  
### 9:45 AM - Daily Planning
- [ ] **Prioritize today's tasks**
  - Urgent: Campaign issues, negative reviews, support backlog
  - Important: Scheduled content, report deadlines
  - Strategic: A/B tests, new campaign ideas
  
- [ ] **Review content calendar**
  - Confirm scheduled posts are ready
  - Identify gaps for organic content opportunities
  
- [ ] **Brief update to Technical Lead** (if needed)
  - Send quick morning summary via Slack/WhatsApp
  - Flag any blockers or urgent issues

## Active Hours (10:00 AM - 5:00 PM)

### Core Responsibilities (Throughout Day)

#### 🔴 Priority 1: WhatsApp Customer Support
**Target:** <10 minute response time during peak hours (7 AM - 8 PM)

**10:00 AM - 12:00 PM** (Morning Peak)
- [ ] Monitor WhatsApp Business continuously
- [ ] Use quick reply templates for common questions
- [ ] Log complex issues in support tracker
- [ ] Peak volume: 15-30 messages

**Common Request Types:**
- App installation help (25%)
- Booking assistance (30%)
- Payment issues (20%)
- Route/schedule inquiries (15%)
- General feedback (10%)

**Response Protocol:**
1. Acknowledge within 2 minutes with greeting
2. Provide solution or ETA for resolution
3. Confirm customer satisfaction
4. Log in daily report

#### 🟡 Priority 2: Campaign Management

**10:30 AM - Check Campaign Performance**
- [ ] Meta Ads: Review morning metrics
  - If CPI trending >$0.55: Pause underperforming ad sets
  - If CTR <1%: Refresh creative or copy
  - If frequency >3: Expand audience or rotate ads
  
- [ ] Google Ads: Monitor UAC performance
  - Check install volume pacing
  - Adjust bids if needed for target CPI
  - Review search term insights

**Campaign Optimization Actions:**
- **Daily:** Pause poor performers, adjust budgets
- **2-3x/week:** Test new creatives or audiences
- **Weekly:** Comprehensive campaign restructure if needed

#### 🟢 Priority 3: Content Creation & Social Media

**11:00 AM or 2:00 PM** (Flexible based on support volume)

**Content Creation (2-3x per week):**
- [ ] Create 2-3 social media posts using Canva
  - Mix: 40% educational, 30% promotional, 30% engagement
  - Include testimonials from field team
  - Use high-quality terminal/bus images
  
- [ ] Write engaging captions
  - Keep under 150 characters for Facebook
  - Use relevant hashtags: #TransConnectUG #TravelSmart #KampalaTransport
  - Include clear CTA: "Download now" or "Book your seat"
  
- [ ] Schedule posts in Meta Business Suite
  - Best times: 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 5-7 PM
  - Avoid posting during low-engagement hours (9 PM - 6 AM)

**Social Engagement (Daily):**
- [ ] Respond to all comments within 2 hours
- [ ] Reply to DMs within 30 minutes
- [ ] Share user-generated content (with permission)
- [ ] Monitor competitor pages for insights

#### 📊 Priority 4: Monitoring & Optimization

**Throughout Day:**
- [ ] Check campaign metrics every 2 hours
- [ ] Monitor WhatsApp message queue
- [ ] Respond to Play Store reviews as they come in
- [ ] Track daily install count vs. goal

**Mid-Day Check (12:30 PM):**
- [ ] CPI status: On track for <$0.50?
- [ ] Install volume: Pacing toward daily goal?
- [ ] Support: Response time averaging <10 min?

- Take a proper break
- Set WhatsApp status: "Away - Back at 2:00 PM"
- Check messages upon return

## Afternoon Focus (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM)

### 2:00 PM - Resume Active Monitoring
- [ ] Clear any support backlog from lunch
- [ ] Review campaign performance changes
- [ ] Execute planned content tasks

### 3:00 PM - Deep Work Block
Choose one strategic task:

**Option A: Campaign Development**
- Research new audience segments
- Design A/B test variations
- Plan next week's campaigns

**Option B: Content Production**
- Create batch content for next 3-5 days
- Record video testimonials (coordinate with field team)
- Update ad creative library

**Option C: Analysis & Strategy**
- Analyze conversion funnel drop-offs
- Identify top-performing creative/copy
- Develop recommendations for Technical Lead

### 4:00 PM - Field Team Coordination
- [ ] Check in with Field Operations Lead
  - Request photos/videos from terminals
  - Get feedback on customer questions/concerns
  - Coordinate on promotional activities
  
- [ ] Share digital performance insights
  - Which routes/buses getting most interest online
  - Common customer questions to address in field
  - Testimonials needed for content

### 4:30 PM - Final Support Push
- [ ] Address any pending WhatsApp inquiries
- [ ] Respond to afternoon social media activity
- [ ] Close or escalate open support tickets

---

## End-of-Day (5:00 PM - 5:30 PM)

### 5:00 PM - Metrics Logging
- [ ] **Record daily KPIs** in tracking sheet:
  - Total app installs (from analytics)
  - Average CPI across all campaigns
  - WhatsApp messages received
  - Average response time
  - Social media engagement (likes, comments, shares)
  - New bookings attributed to your channels
  
- [ ] **Flag issues for tomorrow**
  - Underperforming campaigns to optimize
  - Pending customer escalations
  - Content creation tasks
  
### 5:15 PM - Daily Report
Send brief update to Technical Lead via Slack/WhatsApp:

**Template:**
```
Daily Update - [Date]

📊 Metrics:
• Installs: [X] (CPI: $[Y])
• WhatsApp: [X] messages (Avg response: [Y] min)
• Social: [X] engagements

✅ Wins: [1-2 highlights]
⚠️ Issues: [Any blockers or concerns]
🎯 Tomorrow: [Top priority]
```

### 5:25 PM - Prepare for Next Day
- [ ] Schedule any overnight auto-responses (if applicable)
- [ ] Set campaign budgets for next day
- [ ] Review tomorrow's content calendar
- [ ] Close all dashboards and log out

## Emergency Protocols

### High Priority Escalations (Immediate)
- App completely down or crashing
- Payment system failure
- Negative viral content or PR crisis
- Legal complaints or threats
- Data breach concerns

**Action:** Call Technical Lead immediately + WhatsApp message

### Medium Priority Escalations (Same Day)
- Multiple users reporting same issue
- Campaign CPI suddenly >$1.00
- Play Store rating drops below 3.5 stars
- Competitor launching major campaign

**Action:** WhatsApp message to Technical Lead + schedule call

### Standard Escalations (Within 24 Hours)
- Technical bug affecting <5 users
- Refund requests >50,000 UGX
- Partnership inquiries from large companies
- Feature requests from multiple users

**Action:** Document in tracker + mention in daily update

## Pro Tips for Success

### Efficiency Hacks
- **Template Everything:** WhatsApp responses, social captions, reports
- **Batch Similar Tasks:** Create multiple posts at once, respond to reviews together
- **Use Shortcuts:** Keyboard shortcuts for WhatsApp, browser tabs for dashboards
- **Set Timers:** Use Pomodoro (25 min focus) for deep work tasks

### Avoid Common Pitfalls
- ❌ Don't check campaigns obsessively (every 15 min) - causes over-optimization
- ❌ Don't neglect WhatsApp to focus on campaigns - support comes first
- ❌ Don't create content without data - always check what performed well
- ❌ Don't miss daily logging - impacts weekly report accuracy

### Build Your Rhythm
- **Week 1-2**: Follow schedule strictly to build habits
- **Week 3-4**: Adapt timing based on your peak productivity hours
- **Month 2+**: Develop intuition for when to prioritize what
