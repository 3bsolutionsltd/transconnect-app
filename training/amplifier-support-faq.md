## 3. Booking Process

### Q: How do I book a bus ticket?
**A:** Booking takes just 5 easy steps:

**Step 1: Search**
- Open TransConnect app
- Enter: From, To, Date
- Tap "Search Buses"

**Step 2: Choose Bus**
- See all available buses and times
- Compare prices and bus companies
- Select your preferred departure

**Step 3: Select Seat**
- View seat map
- Tap available seat (green)
- Window or aisle - your choice!

**Step 4: Passenger Details**
- Confirm your name and phone
- Add passengers if booking for others

**Step 5: Payment**
- Choose Mobile Money (MTN/Airtel) or Card
- Complete payment
- Receive QR ticket instantly!

Total time: ~3 minutes. Much faster than going to the terminal! 🚀

---

### Q: How far in advance can I book?
**A:** You can book tickets **up to 14 days in advance**.

**Example:**
- Today: July 10
- You can book through: July 24

**Why this limit?**
Bus schedules can change, so we keep the booking window manageable to ensure accuracy.

**Peak season tip:** Book early! Popular routes fill up fast on weekends and holidays.

---

### Q: Can I book for today/same-day travel?
**A:** Yes! Book right up until **30 minutes before departure** (if seats available).

**But we recommend:**
- Book at least 2 hours ahead for peace of mind
- Peak times (7-9 AM, 5-7 PM) fill up fast
- Allows time for any payment issues

**Already at terminal?** You can still book via app while waiting! Faster than the ticket counter line.

---

### Q: How do I choose a good seat?
**A:** Here's our seat selection guide:

**Seat Map Colors:**
- 🟢 Green = Available (you can book)
- 🔴 Red = Taken (already booked)
- ⚪ Gray = Reserved (driver, crew)

**Best Seats:**

**Window seats (Columns A & D):**
- Great views
- Rest your head
- Good for long journeys

**Aisle seats (Columns B & C):**
- Easy bathroom access
- Stretch your legs
- More personal space

**Front seats (Rows 1-5):**
- Less bumpy ride
- First to exit
- More legroom (usually)

**Back seats (avoid if possible):**
- More bumpy
- Near bathroom
- Hot (engine heat)

**Middle rows (6-10):**
- Balanced comfort
- Not too bumpy
- Safe in crash (statistically)

**Pro tip:** Book early to get your preferred seat!

## 4. Payments

- Fast and reliable

---

### Q: How do I pay with Mobile Money?
**A:** Simple process:

**MTN Mobile Money:**
1. Select "MTN Mobile Money" at payment
2. Enter your MTN number
3. Tap "Pay Now"
4. Wait for USSD prompt: *165#
5. Enter your PIN to approve
6. Wait for confirmation SMS
7. Your ticket arrives within 1 minute!

**Airtel Money:**
1. Select "Airtel Money"
2. Enter your Airtel number
3. Tap "Pay Now"
4. Approve via *185# prompt
5. Enter PIN
6. Receive ticket!

**Important:** You MUST approve the USSD prompt within 2 minutes, or payment times out.

---

### Q: Why did my payment fail?
**A:** Common reasons:

1. **Insufficient Balance**
   - Check: Dial *165# (MTN) or *185# (Airtel)
   - Need ticket price + ~500 UGX transaction fees

2. **Wrong PIN**
   - Double-check your Mobile Money PIN
   - Reset if forgotten: Dial *165# or *185#

3. **Daily Limit Exceeded**
   - Mobile Money has daily transaction limits
   - Default: 1,000,000 UGX/day
   - Try: Tomorrow, or request limit increase

4. **Timeout**
   - Didn't approve USSD within 2 minutes
   - Try: Booking again, approve quickly

5. **Network Issues**
   - Poor Internet or Mobile Money network
   - Try: Better signal area, wait 5 minutes

**Still failing?** Contact us - we'll help troubleshoot!

---

**A:** Yes! Your payment is 100% secure:

🔒 **We DON'T store:**

## 7. Changes & Cancellations

### Q: Can I change my booking?
**A:** Yes, you can modify your booking:

**What You Can Change:**
- Travel date
- Departure time
- Seat number

**Cannot Change:**
- Passenger name (non-transferable)
- Route (must cancel and rebook)

**How to Change:**
1. App → My Bookings
2. Select trip
3. Tap "Modify Booking"
4. Choose what to change
5. Confirm (may have fee)

**Change Fees:**
- Free if changed 24+ hours before
- 10,000 UGX if changed 12-24 hours before
- Not allowed <12 hours before (cancel instead)

---

### Q: How do I cancel my booking?
**A:** Easy cancellation process:

**Steps:**
1. Open TransConnect app
2. Go to "My Bookings"
3. Select trip to cancel
4. Tap "Cancel Booking"
5. Select reason (helps us improve)
6. Confirm cancellation
7. Refund initiated automatically

**Cancellation processed within 1 hour.**
**Refund arrives in 3-5 business days.**

---

### Q: What is your refund policy?
**A:** Our refund policy is fair and transparent:

**Refund Amount Depends on When You Cancel:**

📅 **24+ hours before departure**
- ✅ **100% refund** (minus processing fee)
- Processing fee: 2,000 UGX

📅 **12-24 hours before departure**
- ✅ **50% refund**
- Bus companies lose revenue from late cancellations

📅 **Less than 12 hours before**
- ❌ **No refund**
- Too late for bus to resell seat

**Special Circumstances:**
- Medical emergency (with documentation): Case-by-case
- Bus cancelled by company: 100% refund
- Bus significantly delayed (>2 hours): Partial refund

**No-show (didn't board):** No refund - ticket marked as used.

---

### Q: How long do refunds take?
**A:** **3-5 business days** from cancellation.

**Refund Timeline:**
- Day 1: Cancel booking
- Day 1: Refund approved (instant)
- Day 1-3: Processing by payment provider
- Day 3-5: Money returns to your Mobile Money account

**Refunds go to:**
- Same Mobile Money account used for payment
- Automatically - no action needed from you

**Check refund status:**
- App → My Bookings → View Refund Status

**Not received after 5 days?** Contact us with:
- Booking reference
- Cancellation date
- Payment method used

---
