## Quick Reporting Tips

### Data Collection Throughout Week
- **Daily:** Log installs, CPI, WhatsApp messages in tracker
- **Tuesday/Thursday:** Mid-week campaign checkpoints
- **Friday Morning:** Compile all metrics

### Writing Your Report
- **Be concise:** Use bullets, tables, and visuals
- **Be honest:** Report challenges, not just wins
- **Be actionable:** Every insight should have a recommendation
- **Be forward-looking:** Focus on what's next, not just what happened

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- ❌ Reporting metrics without context or comparison
- ❌ Listing challenges without proposed solutions
- ❌ Generic insights ("ads performed well")
- ❌ Submitting late or incomplete
