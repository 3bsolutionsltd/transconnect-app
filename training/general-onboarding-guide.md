# Onboarding Guide: Field Operations Lead

**Last Updated:** July 2, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Target Role:** Field Operations Lead ("The General")

## Overview

Welcome to TransConnect! As the Field Operations Lead, you are the backbone of our ground operations and the face of TransConnect at bus terminals. This guide will prepare you to lead Brand Ambassadors, build strong relationships with terminal managers and bus operators, execute our branding strategy, and ensure seamless on-ground operations during your first two weeks.

**Your Mission:** Execute TransConnect's on-ground operations strategy with zero operational disruptions while achieving high team productivity and maximum brand visibility at key terminals.

## Leadership Principles for The General

As you build TransConnect's field operations, remember these principles:

**Lead from the Front**
- Be present at terminals regularly
- Model the behavior you expect from Brand Ambassadors
- Never ask your team to do something you wouldn't do

**Build Trust Through Consistency**
- Show up when you say you will
- Follow through on commitments to stakeholders
- Be reliable in good times and challenging times

**Empower Your Team**
- Coach, don't micromanage
- Celebrate wins publicly, address issues privately
- Give Brand Ambassadors ownership of their territories

**Think Long-Term**
- Sustainable partnerships over quick wins
- Quality relationships over quantity
- Build systems that scale beyond your direct involvement

**Communicate Up and Down**
- Keep Technical Lead informed (no surprises)
- Give Brand Ambassadors context for decisions
- Translate strategic vision into field execution

**Adapt and Overcome**
- Terminals are dynamic environments
- What works at Namayiba might not work at Link Bus
- Be flexible while maintaining quality standards
