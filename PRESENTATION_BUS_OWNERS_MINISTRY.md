## 8. RISK MITIGATION (3 minutes)

### Common Concerns & Solutions

**"What about passengers without smartphones?"**
- Web booking portal accessible via stations
- Agent-assisted booking at terminals
- Gradual transition, not forced adoption
- Print QR codes for non-smartphone users

**"Mobile money transaction limits?"**
- Multiple payment options
- Cash payment tracking at stations
- Bank transfer integration coming

**"Internet connectivity issues?"**
- Offline ticket validation capability
- SMS backup for confirmations
- Sync when connection restored

**"Staff resistance to change?"**
- Comprehensive training program
- Dedicated support team
- Gradual rollout approach
- Incentive programs for adoption
