### What is a TransConnect Agent?
As a TransConnect Agent, you serve as the bridge between TransConnect and bus operators. Your role is to:
- **Recruit and register** new bus operators onto the platform
- **Support operators** with onboarding and platform usage
- **Monitor performance** and ensure compliance
- **Drive growth** in your assigned territory

- üì± Mobile app for passengers

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### 1. Operator Registration Problems

**Issue**: "Access denied. You can only manage your own operators."
**Cause**: Authentication token mismatch or expired session
**Solution**:
- Log out and log back in
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Ensure using correct agent credentials
- Contact support if issue persists

**Issue**: Operator registration form won't submit
**Cause**: Missing required fields or invalid data format
**Solution**:
- Check all required fields are completed
- Verify phone number format (+256XXXXXXXXX)
- Ensure email address is valid
- Check business license number format

**Issue**: Operator status stuck in "Pending"
**Cause**: Missing documents or admin backlog
**Solution**:
- Review document checklist
- Contact admin for status update
- Resubmit with complete documentation
- Follow up within 3-5 business days

#### 2. Dashboard Access Issues

**Issue**: Can't log into agent portal
**Cause**: Network connectivity or server issues
**Solution**:
- Check internet connection
- Try different browser or device
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Use incognito/private browsing mode
- Contact IT support if widespread issue

**Issue**: Dashboard loading slowly or not loading
**Cause**: Browser compatibility or network issues
**Solution**:
- Use supported browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Check network connection speed
- Disable browser extensions temporarily
- Try accessing from different network

#### 3. Operator Support Issues

**Issue**: Operator can't access their dashboard
**Cause**: Login credentials or account status issues
**Solution**:
- Verify operator is using correct email/password
- Check if operator account is approved and active
- Guide through password reset process
- Escalate to admin if account is suspended

**Issue**: Operator having trouble with QR scanning
**Cause**: Technical setup or training issues
**Solution**:
- Verify QR scanner app is properly installed
- Check camera permissions on device
- Provide additional training on QR process
- Test with sample QR codes

### Email Best Practices

#### Subject Line Optimization
- **Be specific**: Include their company name
- **Create urgency**: Limited time offers, market changes
- **Mention benefits**: Specific numbers and outcomes
- **Avoid spam triggers**: No ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation
- **Keep it short**: 6-10 words maximum

#### Email Structure Guidelines
- **Opening**: Personal reference or company-specific observation
- **Problem**: Pain point they definitely experience
- **Solution**: Brief overview of TransConnect benefits
- **Proof**: Specific results from similar operators
- **Call to Action**: One clear, specific next step
- **P.S.**: Often the most-read part - use for urgency or additional value

#### Timing Strategy
- **Best Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Times**: 8-10 AM or 1-3 PM
- **Avoid**: Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends
- **Follow-up**: Wait 5-7 business days between emails
