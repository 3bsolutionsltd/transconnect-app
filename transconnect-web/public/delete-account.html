🗑️ Delete Your TransConnect Account

We're sorry to see you go! If you'd like to delete your TransConnect account, please follow the instructions below.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

📋 How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and associated data, please send an email to our support team with the following information:

  1. Subject line: "Delete My Account - [Your Email]"
  2. Email body must include:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your registered phone number
    • Reason for deletion (optional)
📧 Contact Information:
support@transconnect.ug 📱 Response time: Within 48 hours

🔒 What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Personal information: Name, email, phone number, profile picture
  • Booking history: All past and upcoming bookings
  • Payment information: Saved payment methods and transaction history
  • Preferences: Saved routes, favorite operators, notifications settings
  • Authentication: Login credentials and session tokens

📦 Data Retention

Please note:

  • Financial transaction records may be retained for up to 7 years for legal and tax compliance purposes
  • Anonymous analytics data may be retained for service improvement
  • Account deletion is completed within 30 days of your request

🔄 Alternative: Temporarily Disable Account

If you're not sure about permanent deletion, consider temporarily disabling your account:

  1. Open the TransConnect app
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Deactivate Account
  3. Your account will be hidden but data remains recoverable for 60 days

Need help? Contact us at support@transconnect.ug before proceeding with deletion.
