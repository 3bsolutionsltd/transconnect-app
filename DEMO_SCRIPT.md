## DEMO FLOW (8-10 minutes total)

### Part 1: PASSENGER EXPERIENCE (Mobile App) - 3 minutes

**Setup:** Have phone ready with app open to home screen

#### Scene 1: Search & Browse (30 seconds)
**SAY:** *"Let me show you how easy it is for passengers to book a ticket..."*

**DO:**
1. Open TransConnect mobile app
2. Show clean, simple home screen
3. Enter: Origin = "Kampala" | Destination = "Mbarara"
4. Select Date: Tomorrow
5. Tap "Search Buses"

**POINT OUT:**
- "Notice how fast the search is - real-time availability"
- "Passengers can see all operators, prices, and departure times in one place"
- "They're comparing YOUR bus with competitors - you need to be here"

#### Scene 2: Seat Selection (45 seconds)
**SAY:** *"Now watch how passengers choose their seats..."*

**DO:**

2. Show route details (duration, amenities)
3. Tap "Select Seats"

6. Show seat turns green/selected

**POINT OUT:**
- "Green = available, Grey = booked, Blue = selected"
- "Passengers can pick exactly where they want to sit"
- "No more disputes about seat assignments"
- "Real-time updates - if someone else books, it's immediately unavailable"

**SAY:** *"Payment is completely seamless with mobile money..."*

**DO:**
1. Review booking summary (Route, Seat, Price)

4. Tap "Confirm & Pay"
5. Show payment prompt
6. Simulate payment approval

**POINT OUT:**
- "MTN and Airtel Mobile Money integrated"
- "Secure, encrypted transactions"

**SAY:** *"Here's the magic - the digital ticket..."*

**DO:**

## TROUBLESHOOTING DURING DEMO

### If Internet Fails:
- **Plan B**: Use pre-recorded video walkthrough
- **Plan C**: Use prepared screenshots in presentation
- **Messaging**: "Notice even without internet, the QR validation still works offline"

### If App Crashes:
- **Plan B**: Switch to backup device immediately
- **Messaging**: "This is a demo environment - production is more stable. This is exactly why we have a pilot phase."

### If Scanner Won't Scan:
- **Plan B**: Increase phone brightness to maximum
- **Plan C**: Use printed QR code sample
- **Messaging**: "Ensure QR codes are printed clearly, or passengers show full brightness on phone"

### If Questions Interrupt Flow:
- **Response**: "Great question! Let me finish this flow and I'll address it - I have a Q&A section prepared."
- **Alternative**: "I'll show you exactly how that works in the next section."
